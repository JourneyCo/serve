Welcome to the Project Registration System

A centralized platform for managing and participating in various projects. Register for available projects, track your participation, and stay informed about upcoming deadlines.

Key Features

event_available Project Registration

Browse and register for available projects based on your interests and schedule. Receive confirmations and reminders about your registrations.

notifications_active Email Notifications

Stay informed with automated email notifications for registration confirmations and upcoming project deadlines.

dashboard Project Dashboard

Access a personalized dashboard to view your registered projects, upcoming schedules, and participation history.

admin_panel_settings Admin Controls

Administrators can manage projects, user registrations, and system settings through an intuitive admin interface.
